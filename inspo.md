## [withtitan.com](https://withtitan.com) — curated executive-coaching marketplace

What they do: matches Series A–C founders to vetted executive coaches across 5 dimensions (industry, stage, role, coaching style, modality). Hand-curated network ("we interviewed 1,600 coaches so you don't have to"). Stated criteria: $100M+ ARR scaled, $300M+ exits, or 6+ founders coached to unicorns. ~48 coaches listed; founders re-match every 18-24 months as they grow.

**Why this is relevant for Train.** The parallel is closest to Train's curated coach library at [`docs/content/training-styles/`](docs/content/training-styles/) — but Train surfaces it as *content* (style guides ingested into a planner), not as a *marketplace* of humans. Titan suggests there's a richer product surface above the library:

| Titan | Train equivalent / opportunity |
|---|---|
| Curated coach network (humans) | Curated *style library* — each coach's methodology distilled into a guide the planner can use |
| 5-dimension matching | We'd match on goal (jump, oly, marathon), training age, equipment, injury constraints, co-styles |
| Re-match every 18-24 months as athlete progresses | Arc-to-arc style switching — athlete graduates from a strength-priority style to a peaking style at a clear inflection |
| Application-based admission | Quality bar for new coaches entering the library (the ingest-coach pipeline, gated by review) |
| Marketplace economics: affiliate / referral when a founder hires a coach | Affiliate link when an athlete loves the auto-blended plan and wants the coach's paid program |

**Specifically borrowable patterns**:
- **Vetted-not-open.** Titan's whole pitch is the gate. Custom-coach ingestion already separates "global library" (vetted) from "ephemeral per-athlete" (open) — same model.
- **Stage-aware matching.** Don't just match a coach to an athlete once; match by *arc* and re-evaluate at each arc transition.
- **Concrete coach signals.** Titan publishes "scaled $100M ARR" / "coached N unicorns". Train's equivalent: "trained N national-level lifters", "published N programs", "X years coaching reactive power".

**Not borrowable**:
- Their human-coach model is the opposite of Train's "the system replaces the four roles an athlete currently does themselves." Train's promise is *informed but unburdened* — the coach is in the system, not a person you book a call with.
- B2B founder coaching has stronger LTV than B2C athletic coaching. Don't assume the marketplace economics map 1:1.
