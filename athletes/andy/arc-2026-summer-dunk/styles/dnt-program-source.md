## Coach's Notes

1. All accessory exercises (abs, biceps, triceps, etc.): pick a weight allowing 10–12 reps but not exhausted.
2. All accessory exercises should be performed with extra weight (not bodyweight only) — coach leaves load to athlete's discretion.
3. Notation: `6×2` means 6 sets × 2 reps. First number = sets, second = reps. For example, Back Extension `5×10` means 5 sets of 10 reps with a weight allowing 10–12 reps clean.
