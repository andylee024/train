# Block 02 — REACTIVE + DUNK WINDOW

## Purpose

Reactive emphasis: depth jumps enter the program. Dunk attempts begin Wk 9. Bench and pull-up push toward arc targets — Mon adds density (Dylan-style 14-min clock @ 80% Wks 9-11). Squat stays maintenance with PAP complexes introduced (heavy → explosive sequencing).

## Goals

1. **G1**: Approach touch ≥ 3 inches above rim AND first dunk attempts filmed (Wk 9+) (Serves: Arc G1)
2. **G2**: Bench 1RM ≥ 255 lb AND weighted pull-up +50 lb × 5 reps clean (Serves: Arc G2)
3. **G3**: Side split distance reduced ≥ 6 cm cumulative from baseline (Serves: Arc G3)

## Programming Strategy

1. WEEK SHAPE: Sat = peak performance (jump + dunk attempts). Sun stays LIGHT maintenance (only 24h after Sat jump).
2. Sun: TRUE MAINTENANCE squat 3×3 @ 78-80%. NO PAP complex (would compete with Sat reactive work). Posterior chain holds heavy.
3. Bench Mon: Wks 7-8 conventional 5×5 climbing 185→195. Wks 9-11 14-min density clock @ 80% (185 lb), AMRAP-style total reps. Wk 12 test 1RM (target 255).
4. Pull-Up Fri: heavy triples climb 5×3 from +30→+50. Tue 5×3 trails by ~5 lb. Wed 3×8 volume at 60% Fri load.
5. Power Clean Thu: 5×3 wave 75→82% (145→160 lb). Hang Power Clean Sat 4×3 at 72→78%.
6. Hang Snatch Tue: 5×3 wave 60→70% (115→135 lb). DB Push Press 4×4 climbing.
7. Depth Jump (Sat): Wk 7-8 mid box (24 in) 3×5; Wk 9-11 high box (30 in) 3×5; Wk 12 deload 18 in 2×5. Max intent every contact.
8. Dunk attempts begin Wk 9: 5-10 max-intent attempts at end of SATURDAY session, after the depth jumps. Film every attempt.
9. Speed Squat Thu: 5×2 wave 65→72% (240→265 lb). Jump Squat 4×4 @ 30→35%.
10. Sprints: Sat 4×20yd + 2×30yd (jump day); Tue 4×20yd + 1×40yd finisher.
11. RDL/SLDL: wave continues +10 lb/wk from B1 endpoint. 235→275 lb.
12. Wk 7-8 RPE 7-8 (build). Wk 9-11 RPE 8-9 (peak). Wk 12 deload (60% volume).

## Split (6 Workouts / Week, Wed Optional)

1. Sun: Lower Strength (LIGHT maintenance) + Posterior Chain
2. Mon: Upper Horizontal — Bench Density + Row
3. Tue: Speed-Strength B — Olympic Variety + Sprint Finisher
