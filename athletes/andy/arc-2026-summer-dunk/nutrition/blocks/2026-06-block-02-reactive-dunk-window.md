# Block 02 — REACTIVE + DUNK WINDOW (Nutrition)

## Header
- Block: B2 of 3 — Reactive + Dunk Window
- Weeks: W7–W12 (Jun 14 – Jul 25, 2026)
- Pairs with: `training/blocks/2026-06-block-02-reactive-dunk-window.md`
- Kcal phase: −300 deficit

## Purpose
Continue the cut while the dunk window opens. Bw drop multiplies vert as reactive gains land — every pound off is more vert per pound of force. Also the block where cooking expands: with the eating habit established in B1, B2 unlocks a second batch day for variety.

## Goals
- Bw at end of block: ~185 lb (down from 188)
- Kcal target: ~2400 kcal/day
- Protein floor: ≥190g/day
- Adherence target: 80% on-plan
- Calibration: re-check the kcal target at Wk 8 (post-deload) — adaptive thermogenesis can shift maintenance ~100 kcal lower over the long cut

## Strategy
B2 is where the cut compounds. The reactive gains from depth jumps + dunk attempts (from training plan W9 onward) only land if bw is dropping — every pound off shows up as approach-jump height. Eating volume stays where B1 left it (~2400 kcal), but cooking variety expands: a second batch day on Wednesday unlocks ground beef + salmon weeknight options. This prevents the "I'm sick of D1" boredom that kills B1 adherence.

## Weekly Structure
**Cooking pattern: 2 batches/week (Sun + Wed)**

Two cook sessions: Sunday (the main batch) + Wednesday evening (a smaller second batch). The week now has more variety because each batch covers ~3 days of leftovers.

Practical cadence:
- **Sunday evening:** batch one heavy dinner (D1 or D2 — chicken or salmon) — 4 portions
- **Mon-Wed:** Sun batch leftovers + Layer 1 + L1/L6 lunches
- **Wednesday evening:** second batch (D3 ground beef pan, M effort, ~12 min) — 3 portions
- **Thu-Sat:** Wed batch leftovers + post-training banana/whey
- **Total kitchen time:** ~70 min/week (still well below daily-cook overhead)

## Standing Recipes (most-likely picks this block)

B2's pattern unlocks more recipes — orchestrator picks from these 7:

- **D1** sheet-pan chicken thighs + sweet potato + broccoli — L effort, heavy carb (Sunday batch option)
- **D2** sheet-pan salmon + sweet potato + green beans — M effort, balanced (Sunday alternative — variety from chicken)
- **D3** ground beef pan + rice + frozen broccoli — M effort, heavy carb (Wednesday batch workhorse)
- **D6** sheet-pan turkey meatballs + rice + green beans — L effort, heavy carb (Sunday alternative)
- **D7** chicken stir-fry (fresh peppers + broccoli + rice + soy/sesame) — M effort, heavy carb (occasional Wed cook)
- **L1** rotisserie chicken bowl — Q effort, heavy carb (lunch backup)
- **L8** leftover dinner — Q effort (default lunch when batches are fresh)

## Heavy/Light Day Fueling

- **Heavy days (Thu Speed-Strength A, Sat Jump Lab + Dunk Attempts from W9):** heavy-carb dinner (D3, D6, D7 leftover), B5 oatmeal+whey breakfast, post-training banana + 1 scoop whey
- **Sat dunk attempt days (W9+):** extra carbs at Fri dinner (rice ×1.5) and Sat breakfast — performance fueling matters more once the goal is live
- **Light days (Wed rest, Sun light squat):** smaller portions, L3 cottage cheese as a default lunch
- **Post-training (training days only):** 1 banana + 1 scoop whey, non-negotiable on Thu and Sat

## Calibration Moments

- **Wk 8 (≈ 2026-07-04):** post-deload calibration check per arc.md Testing/Calibration. After 8 weeks of cumulative cutting, true maintenance often drops ~100 kcal due to adaptive thermogenesis. Validate the 2400 kcal target — may need to drop to 2300 to maintain loss rate.
- **Wk 10:** mid-block strength check. Squat ≥360 lb? Bench progressing toward 255? If lifts are dropping, halve deficit.
- **Wk 12 (end of B2):** target bw 185 lb. Bench 1RM test (target 255). Transition to B3: **stop cutting**, switch to maintenance.

## Transition (from B1 → B2)

- **Cooking pattern:** unlocks 2nd batch day (Wed evening). Variety expands from 5 to 7 standing recipes.
- **Same kcal target:** still 2400/day — the cut continues at the same rate.
- **Same protein floor:** still 190g/day.
- **New: Sat dunk-attempt fueling.** From W9, Saturday is a true performance day — eat extra carbs Fri dinner + Sat breakfast.
