# Week 2026-W17

## Week Header

- Block: plans/blocks/2026-04-block-02-reset-strength-base-i.md
- Block Week Number: 1
- Week Type: re-entry
- Primary Focus: rebuild consistency, conservative loading, establish baselines

## Weekly Goals

1. Complete 5 training sessions this week — consistency is the win.
2. Re-establish squat and hinge patterns at ~72% intensity.
3. Capture approach touch height baseline at current bodyweight.
4. Begin cut nutrition protocol.
5. Log all sessions via NanoClaw.

## Weekly Metrics Targets

1. Back Squat: complete 4x5 @ 72% with smooth reps.
2. Jump: capture best-of-5 approach touch measurement.
3. Shoulder: track pain level 1-10 after each upper session.
4. Bodyweight: establish 7-day average starting point (~192 lb).

## Strategy For The Week

1. Emphasis: movement quality and getting back into rhythm.
2. Keep: all loads conservative, leave 2-3 reps in reserve on everything.
3. Avoid: chasing old numbers or adding extra volume.

## 5-Day Split Overview

1. Day 1: Lower A (Squat heavy + hinge)
2. Day 2: Upper A (Pull-dominant + shoulder-safe pressing)
3. Day 3: Lower B (SSB Squat + posterior chain)
4. Day 4: Upper B (Row/chin focus + rehab)
5. Day 5: Basketball/Jump

## Day 1 - Lower A

1. Trap Bar Jumps: 4x3 @ 30% (Rest: 90s)
2. Back Squat: 4x5 @ 72% (Rest: 120-150s)
3. RDL: 3x6 @ RPE 7 (Rest: 90-120s)
4. Hand-Supported Bulgarian Split Squat: 3x6/side (Rest: 75-90s)
5. Seated Hamstring Curl: 3x10 (Rest: 60-75s)
6. Side Split Push-Ups: 3x8 (Rest: 45-60s)
7. Straddle Leg Lifts: 3x8 (Rest: 45-60s)
8. Session Note: conservative. Groove the squat pattern, don't grind.

## Day 2 - Upper A

1. Neutral-Grip DB Floor Press: 4x8 @ RPE 7 (Rest: 90s)
2. Weighted Chin-up/Pull-up: 4x5 @ RPE 7 (Rest: 90-120s)
3. Chest Supported Row: 4x8 (Rest: 75-90s)
4. Face Pulls: 3x15 (Rest: 60s)
5. Band Pull-Aparts: 3x20 (Rest: 45s)
6. Dynamic Kneeling Hip Flexor Stretch: 2x10/side (Rest: 30s)
7. Wall Hip Flexor PNF: 2x45s/side (Rest: 30s)
8. Session Note: upper days are lighter during the cut. Prioritize pull volume and shoulder health.

## Day 3 - Lower B

1. KB Swings (heavy): 5x8 (Rest: 90s)
2. SSB Squat or Goblet Squat: 4x5 @ RPE 7-8 (Rest: 120s)
3. Clean Deadlift (straps, no hook grip): 3x4 @ RPE 7 (Rest: 120s)
4. Glute Ham Raises or Nordic Hamstring Curl: 3x6-8 (Rest: 75-90s)
5. Calf Raises: 3x12-15 (Rest: 60s)
6. Horse Stance Squats: 3x8 (Rest: 60s)
7. ISO Side Split Reps: 3x5 (5s holds) (Rest: 45-60s)
8. Session Note: SSB or goblet squat keeps quad stimulus without wrist loading.

## Day 4 - Upper B

1. Landmine Press (single arm): 3x8/side @ RPE 7 (Rest: 75-90s)
2. Barbell Row: 4x6 (Rest: 90-120s)
3. Lat Pulldowns: 3x10 (Rest: 75-90s)
4. Dips: 3x6-8 — ONLY if shoulder is pain-free, otherwise push-ups (Rest: 75-90s)
5. Cable External Rotations: 3x12/side (Rest: 45-60s)
6. Loaded Hip Flexor Lifts: 2x10/side (Rest: 45-60s)
7. Front Split CRAC: 2x30s/side (Rest: 30s)
8. Session Note: test dips cautiously. If any shoulder pain, drop them and add more rows.

## Day 5 - Basketball/Jump

1. Sprint: 3x20 yd, 2x30 yd, 2x40 yd (Rest: ~60s per 10 yd)
2. Seated Vertical Jumps: 4x3 (Rest: 60-90s)
3. Triple Broad Jumps: 3x1 (Rest: 90s)
4. Approach Jumps / Dunk Attempts: 15-18 attempts (quality only) (Rest: 60-90s)
5. Box Jumps (high box): 3x3 (Rest: 90s)
6. Wall Ref. Horse Stance ISO: 2x60s (Rest: 60s)
7. Elevated Pigeon Good Morning: 2x8/side (Rest: 45s)
8. Session Note: capture approach touch height for baseline. You're jumping well — document it.
