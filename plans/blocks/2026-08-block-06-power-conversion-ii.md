# Block 06 - Aug '26 - Power Conversion II

## Goals

1. Continue improving jump expression and approach timing.
2. Raise reactivity while maintaining core strength outputs.
3. Keep Olympic lifts explosive with lower total fatigue.
4. Maintain side-split and hip flexor gains.

## Metrics

1. Approach touch: +4 in vs March baseline.
2. Dunk attempts: improved quality and consistency.
3. Back squat speed at 70-75% improves (subjective bar speed + clean reps).
4. Side split: another 1 cm hip-to-floor improvement.

## Strategy

1. Same key exercises as July with lower volume, higher quality.
2. Prioritize fast reps and full-rest jumps.
3. Keep one primary basketball/jump day plus one primer.
4. Avoid fatigue spikes.

## Split (5 Workouts / Week)

1. Day 1 - Lower A (Power Clean + dynamic squat)
2. Day 2 - Upper A (strength maintenance)
3. Day 3 - Lower B (Front Squat + jump primer)
4. Day 4 - Upper B (press/pull maintenance)
5. Day 5 - Basketball + Jump Day (highest priority)
