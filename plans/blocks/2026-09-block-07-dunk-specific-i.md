# Block 07 - Sep '26 - Dunk Specific I

## Goals

1. Prioritize basketball takeoff skill and dunk execution.
2. Convert existing force/speed into sport-specific jump outcomes.
3. Maintain strength with minimal effective dose.
4. Keep flexibility progression alive.

## Metrics

1. Dunk makes: >=1 make out of 20 attempts by block end.
2. Approach touch: maintain or improve vs August peak.
3. Back Squat: maintain >=90% of August working loads.
4. Side split: hold or improve depth by 0.5-1 cm.

## Strategy

1. Keep gym work simple and repeatable.
2. Shift weekly quality budget toward basketball/jump outcomes.
3. Keep heavy lower lifts low volume but high intent.
4. Protect freshness for Day 5.

## Split (5 Workouts / Week)

1. Day 1 - Lower A (Power Clean + Back Squat maintenance)
2. Day 2 - Upper A (Bench/Pull maintenance)
3. Day 3 - Lower B (Front Squat + low-volume jump primer)
4. Day 4 - Upper B (press/pull maintenance)
5. Day 5 - Basketball + Dunk Session (primary adaptation)
