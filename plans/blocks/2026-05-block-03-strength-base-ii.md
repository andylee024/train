# Block 03 - May '26 - Strength Base II

## Goals

1. Push max-force production in squat and hinge patterns.
2. Improve front squat and weighted pull-up outputs.
3. Keep Olympic lifts present but secondary.
4. Maintain side-split progress while loads rise.

## Metrics

1. Back Squat e1RM: >=400 lb.
2. Front Squat top single: >=145 kg.
3. Weighted Chin-up/Pull-up 5RM: +5-10 lb vs Block 02.
4. Power Clean top double: +5 lb vs Block 02.
5. Side split: hip-to-floor improves by 1 cm.

## Strategy

1. Keep nearly all exercises unchanged from April.
2. Increase intensity and reduce fluff volume.
3. Lower days drive adaptation; upper days support.
4. Keep one high-quality basketball/jump day.
5. Flexibility stays in every session.

## Split (5 Workouts / Week)

1. Day 1 - Lower A (Power Clean + Back Squat heavy)
2. Day 2 - Upper A (Push Jerk + Bench/Weighted Chin heavy)
3. Day 3 - Lower B (Hang Clean + Front Squat/Hinge heavy)
4. Day 4 - Upper B (Push Press/OHP + rows/dips)
5. Day 5 - Basketball + Jump Day
