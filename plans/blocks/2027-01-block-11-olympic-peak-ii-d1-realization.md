# Block 11 - Jan '27 - Olympic Peak II + D1 Realization

## Goals

1. Take strongest run at Clean & Jerk 95-100 kg.
2. Realize high-end squat strength while minimizing fatigue.
3. Maintain dunk capacity with minimal dose.
4. Keep side-split quality and control.

## Metrics

1. Clean & Jerk: 95-100 kg attempt window.
2. Back Squat e1RM: >=405 lb.
3. Front Squat: stable near best.
4. Dunk rate: maintain October standard.

## Strategy

1. Heavy singles/doubles with low accessory volume.
2. Keep exercise menu stable; intensity is the progression.
3. Build two hard weeks, one realization week, one taper/test week.
4. Keep one short jump exposure weekly.

## Split (5 Workouts / Week)

1. Day 1 - Lower A (Clean & Jerk heavy + Back Squat)
2. Day 2 - Upper A (Jerk support + bench/pull maintenance)
3. Day 3 - Lower B (Power Clean + Front Squat)
4. Day 4 - Upper B (light strength support)
5. Day 5 - Basketball/Jump maintenance + mobility

## Week-by-Week Programming

### Week 1

1. Day 1: Clean & Jerk 7x1 @ 85-90%; Back Squat 4x2 @ 84%; Clean Pull 3x2 @ 105%; Side Split Push-Ups 3x8.
2. Day 2: Push Jerk 4x2 @ 85%; Bench Press 4x2 @ 84%; Weighted Chin-up/Pull-up 4x3; Row 3x5-6; Front Split CRAC 2x30s.
3. Day 3: Power Clean 4x2 @ 87.5%; Front Squat 4x2 @ 86%; Clean Deadlift 3x2; ISO Side Split Reps 3x5.
4. Day 4: Push Press 3x2 @ 82.5%; BB OHP 3x3 @ 80%; Barbell Row 3x5; Dips 2x5; Hip Flexor Stretch/Pnf 2 rounds.
5. Day 5: Sprint 2x20 yd; Seated Vertical Jumps 2x3; Approach Jumps/Dunk Attempts 10-14.

### Week 2

1. Day 1: Clean & Jerk 6x1 @ 87.5-92.5%; Back Squat 4x2 @ 86%; Clean Pull 3x2 @ 107-110%; Side Split Push-Ups 3x8.
2. Day 2: Push Jerk 4x2 @ 87.5%; Bench Press 4x2 @ 86%; Weighted Chin-up/Pull-up 4x3; Row 3x5; Front Split CRAC 2x30s.
3. Day 3: Power Clean 4x2 @ 90%; Front Squat 4x2 @ 88%; Clean Deadlift 3x2; ISO Side Split Reps 3x5.
4. Day 4: Push Press 3x2 @ 85%; BB OHP 3x2-3 @ 82.5%; Barbell Row 3x5; Dips 2x5; Hip Flexor Stretch/Pnf 2 rounds.
5. Day 5: Sprint 2x20 yd; Seated Vertical Jumps 2x3; Approach Jumps/Dunk Attempts 10-14.

### Week 3 (Realization)

1. Day 1: Clean & Jerk work to 3-5 heavy singles in 90-95% range (no misses >2), then 2x1 @ 85%; Back Squat 3x2 @ 82%; Side Split Push-Ups 2x8.
2. Day 2: Push Jerk 3x2 @ 80%; Bench Press 3x2 @ 80%; Weighted Chin-up/Pull-up 3x3; Row 2x5-6; Front Split CRAC 2x30s.
3. Day 3: Power Clean 3x2 @ 85%; Front Squat 3x2 @ 82%; Clean Deadlift 2x2; ISO Side Split Reps 2x5.
4. Day 4: Very light upper primer + mobility.
5. Day 5: Light jump maintenance and recovery mobility.

### Week 4 (Peak Test)

1. Day 1: Clean & Jerk test day (goal: 95-100 kg attempt range); Back Squat 2x2 @ 70% after test.
2. Day 2: Bench and pull-up maintenance (very light, 20-30 min).
3. Day 3: Back Squat e1RM check via 1xAMRAP @ 80% (stop RPE 9) + Front Squat 2x2 @ 70%.
4. Day 4: Mobility and recovery only.
5. Day 5: Dunk maintenance test: 20 attempts, record makes + best touch.
