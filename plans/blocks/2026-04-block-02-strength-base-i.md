# Block 02 - Apr '26 - Strength Base I

## Goals

1. Build lower-body force production (squat/hinge) without dropping jump quality.
2. Raise relative strength while continuing controlled body comp improvement.
3. Keep Olympic lift frequency high and slightly heavier than Block 01.
4. Continue side-split progression with the same mobility menu.

## Metrics

1. Back Squat e1RM: reach or exceed 390 lb by end of block.
2. Front Squat: top triple improves from Block 01 Week 3 baseline.
3. Power Clean: top double improves by 5-10 lb from Block 01 Week 3.
4. Side split: another 1-2 cm hip-to-floor improvement across block.
5. Dunk readiness: maintain or improve approach touch from Block 01.

## Strategy

1. Keep almost all exercises the same as March to preserve focus.
2. Increase intensity, reduce unnecessary volume.
3. Make lower days the primary adaptation drivers; upper days support.
4. Keep jump/basketball day high quality, not high fatigue.
5. Keep flexibility work scheduled inside sessions to ensure adherence.

## Split (5 Workouts / Week)

1. Day 1 - Lower A (Power Clean + Back Squat heavy)
2. Day 2 - Upper A (Push Jerk + Bench/Chin heavy)
3. Day 3 - Lower B (Hang Power Clean + Front Squat/Hinge heavy)
4. Day 4 - Upper B (Push Press/OHP + heavy pulls)
5. Day 5 - Basketball + Jump Day (speed/power quality + dunk attempts)
