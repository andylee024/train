# Block 05 - Jul '26 - Power Conversion I

## Goals

1. Convert strength base into faster jump outputs.
2. Improve sprint quality and approach jump rhythm.
3. Keep Olympic lifts explosive with lower fatigue.
4. Maintain side-split progress.

## Metrics

1. Approach touch: +2 in vs March baseline.
2. Sprint: best 20 yd time improves vs Week 1.
3. Power Clean bar speed and top double quality improve.
4. Dunk attempts: improved takeoff quality and consistency.

## Strategy

1. Keep same exercise menu, shift intent to speed.
2. Drop unnecessary volume to preserve neural freshness.
3. Use Lower B + Basketball day for primary jump stimulus.
4. Keep heavy lifts but no grinding reps.

## Split (5 Workouts / Week)

1. Day 1 - Lower A (Power Clean speed + Back Squat speed-strength)
2. Day 2 - Upper A (Bench/Pull strength maintenance)
3. Day 3 - Lower B (Front Squat + hinge + jump primer)
4. Day 4 - Upper B (OHP/rows/dips maintenance)
5. Day 5 - Basketball + Jump Day (primary power expression)
