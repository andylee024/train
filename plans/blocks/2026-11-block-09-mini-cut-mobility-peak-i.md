# Block 09 - Nov '26 - Mini-Cut + Mobility Peak I

## Goals

1. Push body composition toward lean target.
2. Drive side-split depth and kicking range.
3. Maintain strength and jump outputs.
4. Keep Olympic skill exposures.

## Metrics

1. Body comp: body fat in 11-12% range (or waist down ~2.5 in vs March).
2. Bodyweight trend: 0.5-1.0 lb/week loss.
3. Side split: hip-to-floor improves 2 cm.
4. Performance retention: approach touch and squat loads maintained.

## Strategy

1. Keep lifting quality, reduce fatigue and junk volume.
2. Increase dedicated mobility volume but keep sessions short and repeatable.
3. Keep one quality basketball day.
4. Keep protein/recovery high while cutting.

## Split (5 Workouts / Week)

1. Day 1 - Lower A (power + squat maintenance + long split work)
2. Day 2 - Upper A (maintenance + split work)
3. Day 3 - Lower B (front squat/hinge + split work)
4. Day 4 - Upper B (maintenance + split work)
5. Day 5 - Basketball + Jump + recovery mobility
