# Block 04 - Jun '26 - Olympic Build I

## Goals

1. Improve clean and jerk technical consistency under moderate-heavy loads.
2. Build clean pull and front squat transfer for future peaks.
3. Maintain lower-body strength while reducing non-essential fatigue.
4. Continue side-split development.

## Metrics

1. Clean & Jerk top single: >=80 kg.
2. Power Clean top double: stable or +2.5-5 lb.
3. Front Squat top triple: stable at or above Block 03.
4. Olympic exposures: 3+ each week.
5. Side split: hip-to-floor improves 1 cm.

## Strategy

1. Keep exercise menu stable and repeat lifts often.
2. Increase Olympic specificity and reduce extra strength volume.
3. Keep one basketball/jump day for transfer and rhythm.
4. Protect recovery by avoiding grinders.

## Split (5 Workouts / Week)

1. Day 1 - Lower A (Clean & Jerk + Back Squat + split)
2. Day 2 - Upper A (Push Jerk + Bench/Pull + split)
3. Day 3 - Lower B (Hang Clean + Front Squat + Clean Pull)
4. Day 4 - Upper B (Push Press/OHP + pull volume)
5. Day 5 - Basketball + Jump + light Oly primer
