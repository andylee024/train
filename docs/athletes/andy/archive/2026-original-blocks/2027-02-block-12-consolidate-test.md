# Block 12 - Feb '27 - Consolidate + Test

## Goals

1. Consolidate gains from the year without excessive fatigue.
2. Run full scorecard testing (dunk, side split, strength, Olympic).
3. Keep body composition and movement quality stable.
4. Exit healthy and ready for next annual cycle.

## Metrics

1. Dunk make rate: sustain target from October/January tests.
2. Side split: hip-to-floor <=2 cm target (or best-year value).
3. Clean & Jerk: retain >=90-95% of January peak.
4. Back Squat and Front Squat: retain >=90% of peak working performance.
5. Body composition: hold lean range.

## Strategy

1. Maintain exercise menu and reduce noise.
2. Use moderate loads, crisp reps, and scheduled testing.
3. Keep one quality basketball/jump day.
4. Keep flexibility frequent and low friction.

## Split (5 Workouts / Week)

1. Day 1 - Lower A (Power Clean + Back Squat + split)
2. Day 2 - Upper A (press/pull maintenance + split)
3. Day 3 - Lower B (Front Squat + hinge + split)
4. Day 4 - Upper B (light support + mobility)
5. Day 5 - Basketball/Jump test-maintain day

## Week-by-Week Programming

### Week 1 (Consolidate)

1. Day 1: Power Clean 5x2 @ 77.5%; Back Squat 4x3 @ 77%; RDL 3x4; Side Split Push-Ups 3x8; Straddle Leg Lifts 3x8.
2. Day 2: Push Jerk 4x2 @ 75%; Bench Press 4x3 @ 77%; Weighted Chin-up/Pull-up 4x3-4; Row 3x6; Front Split CRAC 2x30s.
3. Day 3: Hang Power Clean 4x2 @ 75%; Front Squat 4x3 @ 77%; Clean Deadlift 3x2; ISO Side Split Reps 3x5.
4. Day 4: Push Press 3x2 @ 75%; BB OHP 3x3 @ 75%; Row 3x5; Dips 2x5; Hip Flexor Stretch/Pnf 2 rounds.
5. Day 5: Sprint 2x20 yd, 2x30 yd; Seated Vertical Jumps 3x3; Approach Jumps/Dunk Attempts 14-18.

### Week 2 (Consolidate)

1. Day 1: Power Clean 5x2 @ 80%; Back Squat 4x3 @ 80%; RDL 3x4; Side Split Push-Ups 3x8; Straddle Leg Lifts 3x8.
2. Day 2: Push Jerk 4x2 @ 77.5%; Bench Press 4x3 @ 80%; Weighted Chin-up/Pull-up 4x3-4; Row 3x6; Front Split CRAC 2x30s.
3. Day 3: Hang Power Clean 4x2 @ 77.5%; Front Squat 4x3 @ 80%; Clean Deadlift 3x2; ISO Side Split Reps 3x5.
4. Day 4: Push Press 3x2 @ 77.5%; BB OHP 3x3 @ 77%; Row 3x5; Dips 2x5; Hip Flexor Stretch/Pnf 2 rounds.
5. Day 5: Sprint 2x20 yd, 2x30 yd; Seated Vertical Jumps 3x3; Approach Jumps/Dunk Attempts 16-20.

### Week 3 (Test Week)

1. Day 1: Back Squat e1RM check (1xAMRAP @ 80%, stop RPE 9); Power Clean heavy doubles up to quality top set; side-split measurement.
2. Day 2: Bench/Chin maintenance only + mobility.
3. Day 3: Front Squat quality top single/triple check + Clean & Jerk heavy singles to quality top.
4. Day 4: Recovery upper + mobility flow 20-30 min.
5. Day 5: Dunk Test Session: 20 official attempts + best touch record.

### Week 4 (Transition)

1. Day 1: Full-body light technical day (30-45 min).
2. Day 2: Mobility emphasis (side split and hip flexor, 30-40 min).
3. Day 3: Light lower strength primer (2-3 sets each main lift, 60-65%).
4. Day 4: Light upper strength primer (2-3 sets each main lift, 60-65%).
5. Day 5: Optional fun basketball/jump session at moderate intensity.
