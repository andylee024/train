# Block 01 - Mar '26 - Foundation Cut + Mobility

## Goals

1. Start a controlled cut while keeping lower-body strength stable.
2. Rebuild jump exposure quality (without adding extra days).
3. Make side-split work consistent and measurable.
4. Keep Olympic lifting frequency high with simple technical doses.

## Metrics

1. Bodyweight: 7-day average down by 1-2 lb across the block.
2. Side split: hip-to-floor distance improves by at least 2 cm from Week 1 baseline.
3. Dunk readiness: approach touch best-of-5 improves by 1-2 inches from Week 1 baseline.
4. Strength retention: back squat top working set remains at or above 90% of Week 1 load by Week 4.
5. Olympic consistency: complete 3 Olympic exposures per week, every week.

## Strategy

1. Keep exercise selection fixed for all 4 weeks to reduce complexity.
2. Progress mostly through load and execution quality, not exercise changes.
3. Use one hard jump/basketball day plus one low-volume jump primer in Lower B.
4. Insert flexibility work directly into each session so it is non-optional.
5. Keep effort mostly at RPE 6-8 (no grinders this block).

## Split (5 Workouts / Week)

1. Day 1 - Lower A (Power Clean + Back Squat emphasis + long split work)
2. Day 2 - Upper A (Jerk primer + Bench/Chin emphasis + short split work)
3. Day 3 - Lower B (Hang Clean + Front Squat/Hinge emphasis + long split work)
4. Day 4 - Upper B (Push Press/OHP + pull volume + short split work)
5. Day 5 - Basketball + Jump Day (sprint, jump, dunk attempts + recovery split work)
