## Weekly Goals

1. Complete all 5 sessions with clean technique and no grinders.
2. Establish baseline metrics for approach touch, dunk attempts, and side-split depth.
3. Start controlled bodyweight trend down while keeping performance stable.

## Weekly Metrics Targets

1. Back Squat: complete 4x5 @ 72% with all reps clean.
2. Olympic Lift: Power Clean 6x2 @ 70% completed with consistent bar speed.
3. Jump: 15-20 dunk attempts on Day 5 and best approach touch baseline captured.
4. Flexibility: side-split hip-to-floor baseline measured once this week.
5. Body Composition: 7-day average BW stable to -0.5 lb vs last week.

## Strategy For The Week

1. Emphasis: quality and consistency.
2. Keep: same exercise menu and same session order.
3. Avoid: adding extra accessories or turning jump day into conditioning.

5. Seated Hamstring Curl: 3x10 (Rest: 75-90s)
6. Side Split Push-Ups: 3x8 (Rest: 45-60s)
7. Straddle Leg Lifts: 3x8 (Rest: 45-60s)

## Day 2 - Upper A

1. Push Jerk: 6x2 @ 60-65% (Rest: 90-120s)
2. Bench Press: 4x5 @ 72% (Rest: 150-210s)
3. Weighted Chin-up/Pull-up: 4x5 @ RPE 7 (Rest: 120s)
