## Weekly Goals

1. Complete 5 training sessions — maintain consistency streak.
2. Small loading increase on squat and hinge movements.
3. Reassess shoulder — is floor press and landmine press staying pain-free?
4. Bodyweight trending down from Week 1 baseline.

## Weekly Metrics Targets

1. Back Squat: complete 4x4 @ 75% with good bar speed.
2. Jump: maintain or improve approach touch from Week 1.
3. Shoulder: pain level should be same or improving vs Week 1.
4. Bodyweight: 7-day average should be 0.5-1 lb below Week 1.

## Strategy For The Week

1. Emphasis: small progression on lower body, hold position on upper.
2. Keep: conservative approach to pressing, high pull volume.
3. Avoid: adding sets or exercises. Stay within the template.

## 5-Day Split Overview

1. Day 1: Lower A (Squat heavy + hinge)
2. Day 2: Upper A (Pull-dominant + shoulder-safe pressing)
3. Day 3: Lower B (SSB Squat + posterior chain)
4. Day 4: Upper B (Row/chin focus + rehab)
5. Day 5: Basketball/Jump

## Day 1 - Lower A

1. Trap Bar Jumps: 4x3 @ 30-35% (Rest: 90s)
2. Back Squat: 4x4 @ 75% (Rest: 120-150s)
3. RDL: 3x6 @ RPE 7-8 (Rest: 90-120s)
4. Hand-Supported Bulgarian Split Squat: 3x6/side (Rest: 75-90s)
5. Seated Hamstring Curl: 3x10 (Rest: 60-75s)
6. Side Split Push-Ups: 3x8 (Rest: 45-60s)
7. Straddle Leg Lifts: 3x8 (Rest: 45-60s)
8. Session Note: slight intensity bump. Bar speed should still be crisp.

## Day 2 - Upper A

1. Neutral-Grip DB Floor Press: 4x8 @ RPE 7-8 (Rest: 90s)
2. Weighted Chin-up/Pull-up: 4x5 @ RPE 7-8 (Rest: 90-120s)
3. Chest Supported Row: 4x8 (Rest: 75-90s)
4. Face Pulls: 3x15 (Rest: 60s)
5. Band Pull-Aparts: 3x20 (Rest: 45s)
6. Dynamic Kneeling Hip Flexor Stretch: 2x10/side (Rest: 30s)
7. Wall Hip Flexor PNF: 2x45s/side (Rest: 30s)
8. Session Note: if floor press felt easy last week, add 5 lb per DB.

## Day 3 - Lower B

1. KB Swings (heavy): 5x8 (Rest: 90s)
2. SSB Squat or Goblet Squat: 4x5 @ RPE 8 (Rest: 120s)
3. Clean Deadlift (straps): 3x4 @ RPE 7-8 (Rest: 120s)
4. Glute Ham Raises or Nordic Hamstring Curl: 3x6-8 (Rest: 75-90s)
5. Calf Raises: 3x12-15 (Rest: 60s)
6. Horse Stance Squats: 3x8 (Rest: 60s)
7. ISO Side Split Reps: 3x5 (5s holds) (Rest: 45-60s)
8. Session Note: push SSB load slightly if Week 1 was comfortable.

## Day 4 - Upper B

1. Landmine Press: 3x8/side @ RPE 7-8 (Rest: 75-90s)
2. Barbell Row: 4x6 — add 5 lb if Week 1 was clean (Rest: 90-120s)
3. Lat Pulldowns: 3x10 (Rest: 75-90s)
4. Dips: 3x6-8 — or push-ups if shoulder still limited (Rest: 75-90s)
5. Cable External Rotations: 3x12/side (Rest: 45-60s)
6. Loaded Hip Flexor Lifts: 2x10/side (Rest: 45-60s)
7. Front Split CRAC: 2x30s/side (Rest: 30s)
8. Session Note: if dips were pain-free Week 1, keep them. If not, keep substituting.

## Day 5 - Basketball/Jump

1. Sprint: 3x20 yd, 3x30 yd, 2x40 yd (Rest: ~60s per 10 yd)
2. Seated Vertical Jumps: 4x3 (Rest: 60-90s)
3. Triple Broad Jumps: 4x1 (Rest: 90s)
4. Approach Jumps / Dunk Attempts: 18-22 attempts (Rest: 60-90s)
