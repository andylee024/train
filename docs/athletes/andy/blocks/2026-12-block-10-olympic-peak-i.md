# Block 10 - Dec '26 - Olympic Peak I

## Goals

1. Rebuild and peak heavy Clean & Jerk expression.
2. Maintain sufficient squat strength for C&J support.
3. Keep jump readiness with minimal dose.
4. Preserve mobility gains.

## Metrics

1. Clean & Jerk: >=90 kg.
2. Power Clean: maintain high-quality doubles near yearly best.
3. Front Squat: maintain >=90% of summer top loads.
4. Side split: depth maintained.

## Strategy

1. Olympic singles are primary stressor.
2. Reduce jump volume to one quality day.
3. Keep accessories minimal.
4. Stay fresh enough for quality heavy singles.

## Split (5 Workouts / Week)

1. Day 1 - Lower A (Clean & Jerk heavy + Back Squat)
2. Day 2 - Upper A (Jerk support + upper maintenance)
3. Day 3 - Lower B (Power Clean + Front Squat)
4. Day 4 - Upper B (press/pull maintenance)
5. Day 5 - Basketball/Jump maintenance day

## Week-by-Week Programming

### Week 1

1. Day 1: Clean & Jerk 8x1 @ 80-85%; Back Squat 4x3 @ 80%; Clean Pull 4x2 @ 100% clean; Side Split Push-Ups 3x8.
2. Day 2: Push Jerk 5x2 @ 80%; Bench Press 4x3 @ 80%; Weighted Chin-up/Pull-up 4x3; Row 3x6; Front Split CRAC 2x30s.
3. Day 3: Power Clean 5x2 @ 82.5%; Front Squat 4x3 @ 82%; Clean Deadlift 3x2; ISO Side Split Reps 3x5.
4. Day 4: Push Press 4x2 @ 80%; BB OHP 4x3 @ 80%; Barbell Row 4x5; Dips 3x5; Hip Flexor Stretch/Pnf 2 rounds.
5. Day 5: Sprint 2x20 yd, 2x30 yd; Seated Vertical Jumps 3x3; Approach Jumps/Dunk Attempts 12-16.

### Week 2

1. Day 1: Clean & Jerk 7x1 @ 82.5-87.5%; Back Squat 4x3 @ 82%; Clean Pull 4x2 @ 102-105% clean; Side Split Push-Ups 3x8.
2. Day 2: Push Jerk 5x2 @ 82.5%; Bench Press 4x3 @ 82%; Weighted Chin-up/Pull-up 4x3; Row 3x6; Front Split CRAC 2x30s.
3. Day 3: Power Clean 5x2 @ 85%; Front Squat 4x3 @ 84%; Clean Deadlift 3x2; ISO Side Split Reps 3x5.
4. Day 4: Push Press 4x2 @ 82.5%; BB OHP 4x3 @ 82%; Barbell Row 4x5; Dips 3x5; Hip Flexor Stretch/Pnf 2 rounds.
5. Day 5: Sprint 2x20 yd, 2x30 yd; Seated Vertical Jumps 3x3; Approach Jumps/Dunk Attempts 12-16.

### Week 3

1. Day 1: Clean & Jerk 6x1 @ 85-90%; Back Squat 4x2 @ 84%; Clean Pull 3x2 @ 105-110% clean; Side Split Push-Ups 3x8.
2. Day 2: Push Jerk 4x2 @ 85%; Bench Press 4x2 @ 84%; Weighted Chin-up/Pull-up 4x3; Row 3x5-6; Front Split CRAC 2x30s.
3. Day 3: Power Clean 4x2 @ 87.5%; Front Squat 4x2 @ 86%; Clean Deadlift 3x2; ISO Side Split Reps 3x5.
4. Day 4: Push Press 4x2 @ 85%; BB OHP 4x2-3 @ 84%; Row 4x5; Dips 3x5; Hip Flexor Stretch/Pnf 2 rounds.
5. Day 5: Sprint 2x20 yd; Seated Vertical Jumps 2x3; Approach Jumps/Dunk Attempts 10-14.

### Week 4 (Deload / Heavy Single Check)

1. Day 1: Clean & Jerk work to 1-3 heavy singles (not max grind), then 3x1 @ 80%; Back Squat 3x2 @ 72%; Side Split Push-Ups 2x8.
2. Day 2: Push Jerk 3x2 @ 70%; Bench Press 3x2 @ 72%; Pull-up 3x3 light; Row 2x6.
3. Day 3: Power Clean 3x2 @ 75%; Front Squat 3x2 @ 75%; ISO Side Split Reps 2x5.
4. Day 4: Very light upper primer 20-30 min + mobility.
5. Day 5: Light jump maintenance and recovery flow 15-20 min.
