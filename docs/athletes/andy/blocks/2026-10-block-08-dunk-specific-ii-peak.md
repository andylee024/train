# Block 08 - Oct '26 - Dunk Specific II (Peak)

## Goals

1. Peak dunk expression and approach jump output.
2. Keep neural freshness high.
3. Maintain essential strength and Olympic speed.
4. Preserve flexibility gains.

## Metrics

1. Dunk makes: >=3 makes out of 20 attempts.
2. Approach touch: best yearly value or within 1 in of best.
3. Strength maintenance: no >10% drop in key working loads.
4. Side split: depth maintained.

## Strategy

1. Reduce gym fatigue and keep quality high.
2. Keep exercise selection unchanged but trim volume.
3. Prioritize jump intent and dunk skill.
4. Use Week 4 as a peak/test week.

## Split (5 Workouts / Week)

1. Day 1 - Lower A (low-volume power + squat)
2. Day 2 - Upper A (maintenance)
3. Day 3 - Lower B (jump primer + front squat)
4. Day 4 - Upper B (maintenance)
5. Day 5 - Basketball + Dunk Peak Session

## Week-by-Week Programming

### Week 1

1. Day 1: Power Clean 4x2 @ 80%; Back Squat 4x2 @ 78%; RDL 2x4; Side Split Push-Ups 3x8; Straddle Leg Lifts 3x8.
2. Day 2: Push Jerk 4x2 @ 75%; Bench Press 4x2 @ 80%; Weighted Chin-up/Pull-up 4x3; Row 3x6; Hip Flexor Stretch/Pnf 2 rounds.
3. Day 3: Hang Power Clean 4x2 @ 77.5%; Front Squat 4x2 @ 80%; Seated Vertical Jumps 3x3; ISO Side Split Reps 3x5.
4. Day 4: Push Press 3x2 @ 77.5%; BB OHP 3x3 @ 77%; Barbell Row 3x5; Dips 3x5; Front Split CRAC 2x30s.
5. Day 5: Sprint 3x20 yd, 2x30 yd; Approach Jumps 8; Dunk Attempts 22; Triple Broad Jumps 2x1; Recovery mobility 15 min.

### Week 2

1. Day 1: Power Clean 4x2 @ 82.5%; Back Squat 4x2 @ 80%; RDL 2x4; Side Split Push-Ups 3x8; Straddle Leg Lifts 3x8.
2. Day 2: Push Jerk 4x2 @ 77.5%; Bench Press 4x2 @ 82%; Weighted Chin-up/Pull-up 4x3; Row 3x6; Hip Flexor Stretch/Pnf 2 rounds.
3. Day 3: Hang Power Clean 4x2 @ 80%; Front Squat 4x2 @ 82%; Seated Vertical Jumps 3x3; ISO Side Split Reps 3x5.
4. Day 4: Push Press 3x2 @ 80%; BB OHP 3x2-3 @ 80%; Row 3x5; Dips 3x5; Front Split CRAC 2x30s.
5. Day 5: Sprint 2x20 yd, 2x30 yd; Approach Jumps 8; Dunk Attempts 24; Triple Broad Jumps 2x1; Recovery mobility 15 min.

### Week 3 (Taper)

1. Day 1: Power Clean 3x2 @ 75%; Back Squat 3x2 @ 72%; Side Split Push-Ups 2x8; Straddle Leg Lifts 2x8.
2. Day 2: Push Jerk 3x2 @ 70%; Bench Press 3x2 @ 75%; Weighted Chin-up/Pull-up 3x3 light; Row 2x6.
3. Day 3: Hang Power Clean 3x2 @ 72%; Front Squat 3x2 @ 75%; Seated Vertical Jumps 3x3; ISO Side Split Reps 2x5.
4. Day 4: Push Press 2x2 @ 72%; BB OHP 2x3 @ 72%; Row 2x5; Dips 2x5; Front Split CRAC 2x20-30s.

### Week 4 (Peak Test)

1. Day 1: Power Clean 3x1 @ 75%; Back Squat 2x2 @ 67%; light mobility.
2. Day 2: Bench Press 2x2 @ 70%; Pull-ups 2x3; light mobility.
3. Day 3: Hang Power Clean 2x1 @ 70%; Front Squat 2x2 @ 67%; 2x3 seated jumps.
4. Day 4: Very light upper primer (20-30 min total).
5. Day 5: Dunk Test Session: warm-up + 20 official dunk attempts; record makes and best touch.
