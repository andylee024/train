Honey Garlic Butter Chicken
Mac n Cheese - 597 Calories
High Protein Honey Garlic Butter Chicken Mac
n Cheese!������

Absolutely one of the best combos, so
indulgent, bursting with flavour and packed
with 60g of protein!

�� Macros Per Serving (4 Total)

   597   Calories
   59g    Protein
   52g    Carbs
   16g   Fat

�� Ingredients (Makes 4)

Honey Garlic Chicken

   800g Raw Chicken Breast cut into cubes
   1.5 Tsp Salt
   1 Tsp Black Pepper

   1 Tsp Chilli Flakes (leave it out if you don’t

   want it spicy)
   1.5 Tsp Garlic Powder
   1.5 Tsp Onion Powder
   1.5 Tep Paprika or Smoked Paprika
   2 Tsp Olive Oil
   30g Light Butter (Brand: Lurpak) for
   cooking + extra once cooked
   2 Tsp Minced Garlic
   30g Honey (15g Honey per batch of
   chicken)
   Handful Fresh Chopped Parsley

Garlic Mac n Cheese

   Big Tbsp Minced Garlic
   300ml Skimmed Milk
   160g Light Cream Cheese (Brand:
   Philadelphia)
   1 Tsp Salt or to taste

   1/2 Tsp Black Pepper
   1 Tsp Smoked Paprika

   1/2 Tsp Nutmeg (adds a great deep
   flavour!)
   100g Low Fat Cheddar Cheese (You can
   use any low fat cheese you have available)
   220g Uncooked Macaroni Pasta or any
   pasta of your choice

�� Important Cooking Notes

   Get a pan on medium heat first then add
   butter. Let it melt then add your chicken
   (don’t overcrowd the pan)
   Cook for 4-5 mins on one side first till you
   get a nice golden brown crust. Then cook
   the other side for 3-4 mins more
   Lower the heat completely when adding the
   extra butter, garlic and honey. Mix till well
   coated and sticky then set aside



Checklist


          STEP 1 - First slice your chicken
          breasts into cubes. Season with
          salt, pepper, chilli flakes, garlic
          powder, onion powder, paprika and
          olive oil. Mix till the colour changes
          and set aside


          STEP 2 - Get water in a pot, bring
          to a boil then cook your pasta for 7-
          8 mins. Once cooked drain and set
          aside. You don't want to overcook
          these as they will continue to cook
          in the sauce later



          STEP 3 - Get a pan to medium heat
          then melt some butter and cook the
          chicken bites for 4-5 mins each
          side till they're golden brown and
          crispy


          STEP 4 - Next lower the heat
          completely and add extra butter,
          minced garlic, drizzle of honey and
          freshly chopped parsley. Give the
          chicken a good mix till all pieces
          are well coated then set aside


          STEP 5 - In the residue butter left in
          the pan, on low heat add lots of
          minced garlic. Cook this for 2 mins
          till fragrant then add milk, cream

          cheese and the extra spices


          STEP 6 - Mix this till combined and
          smooth then add grated cheddar
          cheese. Mix till it melts and the
          sauce slightly thickens then add
          your cooked pasta in the sauce


          STEP 7 - Give it a gentle thorough
          mix till fully incorporated. Finally
          you're ready to serve with the juicy
          chicken and your high proteins
          meals are ready for the week!

Crispy Salt N Pepper Chicken &
Chips - 436 Calories
High Protein Crispy Salt n Pepper Chicken &
Chips Meal Prep! Only 436 Calories per serve
������

Combining 2 very popular takeout meals! This
tastes absolutely delicious, is low in calories,
filling and easy to make for the week ahead!

�� Macros per serving (4 Total)

   436    Calories
   40g    Protein
   47g   Carbs
   10g   Fat

�� Ingredients (4 Servings)

   700g Raw Chicken Breast Cubed
   1 tbsp Dark & Low Soy Sauce each

   2 tsp Black Pepper
   2 tsp Garlic Powder
   25-30g Corn Flour / Corn Starch
   800g Raw White Potatoes cut into Chips
   Season Salt, Pepper, Garlic Powder,
   Paprika
   2 tsp Olive Oil
   1 Medium Onion chopped

   1 Medium Red & Green Bell Peppers

   chopped
   Season Salt, Pepper, Garlic, Paprika, Chilli
   Flakes
   20g Honey
   Chopped Green Onion

�� Important Cooking Notes

   Mix the chicken till well combined, you can
   cook the chicken in the air fryer for 10-12
   mins at 200C or cook like the video
   instructions
   Bake the fries in the oven for 25mins at
   210C or
   Air Fry for 12-15mins at 210C
   You can reheat the fries in a pan or air fryer
   to make them cripsy again



Checklist


          STEP 1 - First slice your chicken
          breasts into cubes then add soy
          sauce, black pepper, garlic powder
          and cornflour. Mix till you lose
          patience!


          STEP 2 - Next slice your washed
          potatoes into chips. Place on a
          baking tray, season with salt,
          pepper, garlic powder, paprika and
          olive oil. Mix well and bake or air fry
          on the settings listed above


          STEP 3 - While the chips are
          cooking, get a pan on medium heat
          add cooking spray or olive oil then
          cook the chicken for 8-10 mins till
          golden and crispy then set aside


          STEP 4 - In the same pan add
          chopped onions and bell peppers,
          cook for 2 mins before adding more
          seasoning, honey, the cooked chips
          and green onion. Give everything a
          good mix


          STEP 5 - Then serve with the crispy
          chicken and distribute into 4 equal
          servings then ENJOY!

Creamy Chipotle Steak &
Potatoes - 533 Calories
High Protein Creamy Chipotle Steak &
Potatoes ONLY 533 Calories!������

One of the best meal preps you can make! So
easy and simple - Juicy seasoned steak paired
with creamy chipotle potatoes����

�� Macros Per Serving (4 Servings Total)

   533    Calories
   60g    Protein
   47g    Carbs
   12g   Fat

�� Ingredients (To Make 4 Servings)

   800g Beef Steaks cut into cubes (I use
   Topside / Top Round cut of Beef)
   1.5 Tsp Salt
   1.5 Tsp Garlic Powder
   1.5 Tsp Parsley
   1 Tsp Cumin

   1.5 Tsp Smoked Paprika
   1 Whole Lime Juice

   2 Tsp Olive Oil
   20g Light Butter (For cooking + extra after
   cooking)
   Fresh Chopped Parsley

Chipotle Creamy Potatoes

   800g Raw White Potatoes Cubed
   2 Tsp Italian Herbs
   1.5 Tsp Garlic Powder
   1.5 Tsp Paprika
   1.5 Tsp Salt
   2 Tsp Olive Oil (Optional)
   3-4 Garlic Cloves chopped
   100g Red Onion chopped
   1 Tsp Italian Herbs

   50g Chipotle Chilli Pasta (Brand: Waitrose)
   250ml Light Evaporated Milk or Regular
   Milk
   100g Light Cream Cheese
   30g Grated Parmesan Cheese
   Freshly Chopped Parsley

�� Important Cooking Notes

   Season and marinate your steak bites for at
   least 30 mins for better flavour
   Cook the steak bites on medium high heat
   for 2-3 mins each side in some butter. You
   want to get a nice brown crust
   Don’t overcrowd the pan!
   Bake or air fry the potatoes for 20-22 mins
   at 200C / 400F
   Cook the garlic and onion for 5 mins till soft
   then add the herbs and chipotle paste. Mix
   this in well before adding the milk
   You do not need to cook the sauce, just mix
   it on low heat till smooth then add the

   potatoes



Checklist


          STEP 1 - Start by slicing your
          steaks into bite size cubes then
          season with salt, garlic powder,
          parsley, cumin, smoked paprika,
          lime juice and olive oil. Mix
          thoroughly till well coated


          STEP 2 - Next wash your potatoes,
          slice them into small cubes and
          place on a mixing tray/bowl. Add
          Italian herbs, garlic powder,
          paprika, salt and olive oil. Give
          them a thorough mix till well coated


          STEP 3 - Place them on a lined
          baking tray then oven bake or air
          fry for 20-22 mins at 200C / 400F
          till golden and crispy




          STEP 4 - While the potatoes are
          cooking, get a pan on medium high
          heat. Add butter and cook the steak
          bites for 2 mins each side - you
          want a beautiful brown crust!


          STEP 5 - Once the steaks are
          cooked, lower the heat completely
          down and add extra butter and
          freshly chopped parsley in the pan.
          Give the steaks a good mix till
          coated then set aside


          STEP 6 - In the same pan on
          medium low heat add chopped
          garlic and onion, cook 5 mins till
          soft then add Italian herbs and
          chipotle paste. Give this a mix, add
          milk, cream cheese and grated
          parmesan


          STEP 7 - Mix the sauce till smooth
          and creamy then drop your crispy
          potatoes in and give it a thorough
          mix till well coated. Finally
          distribute and serve equally with
          the steak bites and ENJOY!

Lemon Pepper Chicken Rice
Bowls - 531 Calories
High Protein Lemon Pepper Chicken Rice
Bowls! ONLY 531 Calories������

The flavours in this are absolutely incredible,
so simple and perfect to meal prep for the
week ahead!

�� Macros per Serving (4 Servings Total)

   531   Calories
   46g    Protein
   46g    Carbs
   17g   Fat

�� Ingredients (To make 4 Servings)

Lemon Pepper Chicken

   800g Cubed Chicken Thighs (Skinless,
   Boneless and fat trimmed)
   1.5 Tsp Salt
   2 Tsp Black Pepper (or use lemon pepper
   seasoning)
   2 Tsp Oregano
   1 Tsp Chilli Flakes

   2 Tsp Garlic Powder
   2 Tsp Paprika
   1 Tbsp Lemon Zest

   1 Whole Lemon Juice

   2 Tsp Olive Oil
   15g Light Butter (for cooking)
   Extra 1/2 Lemon Juice
   10-15g Honey

Lemon Garlic Rice

   1 Tbsp Chopped Garlic
   1 Medium Onion Chopped

   1 Tsp Salt

   1 Tsp Paprika

   1 Tsp Turmeric

   1 Tsp Oregano

   200g Uncooked / 540g Cooked Basmati
   Rice
   Chopped Parsley
   1/2 Lemon Juice

Ranch Salad

   150g Fat Free Yogurt
   80g Light Mayo
   1 Tsp Dijon Mustard

   1 Tsp Garlic Powder

   1 Tsp Onion Powder

   1 Tsp Salt & Pepper

   1/2 Lemon Juice
   Parsley
   150g Chopped Tomatoes
   100g Chopped Cucumber

�� Important Cooking Notes

   Marinate the chicken for at least 30 mins, I
   trim off the fat from the thighs
   You can use chicken breast for this too to
   increase protein and lower fat/calories
   Cook the chicken on medium high heat for
   4 mins before turning it over. Should get a
   nice golden crust
   Prep the rice and ranch salad in advance
   Store the ranch salad separately so it’s
   easier to warm up the chicken and rice
   Warm up the chicken and rice in a pan on
   low heat for 5 mins or microwave. Serve
   the ranch salad once it's all warmed up



Checklist


          STEP 1 - Start by trimming the fat
          from the chicken thighs then slicing
          into cubes. Season with salt, black
          pepper, oregano, chilli flakes, garlic
          powder, paprika, lemon zest & juice
          and olive oil


          STEP 2 - Mix really well till the
          colour changes then set aside.
          While that is marinating make the
          white basmati rice to use for later


          STEP 3 - In a bowl add yogurt,
          mayo, dijon mustard, garlic & onion
          powder, salt, pepper, lemon juice
          and parsley. Mix well till smooth
          then add your chopped tomato and
          cucumber. Give it a final mix


          STEP 4 - Now get a pan on medium
          high heat, add light butter then
          cook the chicken for 6-7 mins till
          golden brown. Lower the heat then
          add lemon juice and honey. Give it
          a good mix then set aside



          STEP 5 - In the same pan add
          chopped garlic and onion. Cook for
          5 mins till fragrant then add
          turmeric, paprika, salt and oregano.
          Mix well and cook for another 2
          mins.


          STEP 6 - Finally add your cooked
          basmati rice with fresh parsley.
          Give it a good mix till the colour
          changes to a golden yellow. Add
          lemon juice and mix. Now serve
          with the chicken, salad and ENJOY!

Cheesy Beef Taco Potato Bowls
- 522 Calories

This is without a doubt THE BEST recipe i’ve
made this year so far! The flavours are
INCREDIBLE!

�� Macros Per Serving (4 Total)

   522       Calories
   54g        Protein
   42g        Carbs
   14g       Fat

�� Ingredients (4 Servings)

Taco Seasoning Mix

   1   tsp   Salt
   1   tsp   Black Pepper
   1   tsp   Onion Powder
   1   tsp   Garlic Powder
   1   tsp   Cumin
   1   tsp   Paprika
   1   tsp   Chilli Powder
   1   tsp   Oregano

Potatoes

   800g Raw Cubed Potatoes
   Taco Seasoning Mix
   2 tsp Olive Oil

Chipotle Spicy Sauce

   100g Fat Free Yogurt
   80g Light Mayo (Brand: Hellmans)
   50g Chipotle Chilli Paste (Brand: Waitrose)
   20g Hot Sauce
   20g Honey
   Salt, Paprika, Garlic Powder

Pico de Gallo

   2 Large Tomatoes Chopped
   1 Large Red Onion Chopped

   1/2 Red & Green Bell Peppers Chopped
   Big Handful Coriander chopped
   Salt, Pepper, Garlic, Cumin, Paprika
   1 Whole Lime Juice


Taco Beef

   800g Lean Minced Beef (Topside Beef)
   20g Tomato Paste
   Taco Seasoning Mix
   Grated Mozzarella or any low fat cheese
   15g Grass Fed Butter for cooking

�� Important Cooking Notes

   Season the potatoes thoroughly and mix till
   well coated. Make sure you don’t over cook
   them
   Make the sauce in advance and store in the
   fridge to use for any other meal
   Same thing with the Pico/Salsa, store in a
   container in batches
   Cook the beef till brown then add all the
   ingredients. You can add a little water to
   add moisture if it’s too dry
   Reheat the potatoes and beef only. You can
   store the salsa separately to make it easier



Checklist


              STEP 1 - Slice your potatoes into
              cubes, season with the taco
              seasoning mix and olive oil. Mix
              well and oven or air fry!


              STEP 2 - Next for the chipotle spicy
              sauce, blend yoghurt, mayo,
              chipotle chili paste, hot sauce,
              honey, paprika, garlic powder.
              Blend till smooth & creamy!


              STEP 3 - For the Pico de Gallo, in a
              bowl chop tomatoes, red onion, red
              and green peppers, coriander and
              season with salt, pepper, garlic,
              cumin, paprika and lime juice. Mix
              well and store aside!


              STEP 4 - In a pan add light butter,
              cook the ground beef for 8 mins on
              high heat. Then add tomato paste
              and more taco seasoning. Mix and
              cook for 4 mins and add mozzarella
              on top.


              STEP 5 - Distribute the crispy
              potatoes in equal servings with the
              taco beef and Pico de Gallo.
              Garnish with Chipotle spicy sauce,
              parsley and ENJOY!

Grilled Chicken Alfredo Pasta                      -
595 Calories
High Protein Grilled Chicken Alfredo Pasta!
Only 595 Calories������

One of the most indulgent meal preps i’ve
made! Chicken is cooked to perfection, paired
with a rich creamy Alfredo Pasta. So simple
and easy with great macros����

�� Macros Per Serving (4 Servings Total)

   595    Calories
   59g    Protein
   50g    Carbs
   16g   Fat

�� Ingredients (To Make 4 Servings)

Grilled Chicken

   800g Raw Cubed Chicken Breast
   1 Tsp Salt

   2 Tsp Italian Herbs
   1.5 Tsp Parsley
   1 Tsp Chilli Flakes (remove if you don’t like

   spicy)
   1.5 Tsp Garlic Powder
   1.5 Tsp Paprika
   2 Tsp Olive Oil (you can leave this out too)
   20g Light Butter - Brand: Norpak (For
   cooking + extra once cooked)

Creamy Alfredo Pasta

   210g Uncooked / 520g Cooked Pasta (I
   chose fettuccine)
   80g Chopped White Onion
   4 Garlic Cloves Chopped
   1 Tsp Salt

   1 Tsp Italian Herbs

   1 Tsp Parsley

   0.5 Tsp Chilli Flakes (remove if you don’t
   like spicy)
   1 Tsp Paprika

   150g Tomato Sauce / Passata
   300ml Light Evaporated Milk (Brand:
   Sainsbury’s) or Regular Milk
   130g Light Cream Cheese (Brand:
   Philadelphia)
   30g Grated Parmesan Cheese
   Optional add Pasta Water if the sauce is too
   thick

�� Important Cooking Notes

   Cook the chicken cubes on medium high
   heat, 3-4 mins then turn them over and
   cook for another 3 mins
   Once cooked, lower the heat, add a little
   more butter then mix it all in and set aside
   Cook the onion for 5-6mins till soft then
   add the spices. Mix it in for a min before
   adding the tomato sauce
   You want to let this sauce cook for at least
   5mins on low heat till it bubbles and
   separates a little
   Add the milk and cream cheese on low
   heat, then keep stirring till combined and
   thick
   To reheat, place both chicken and pasta in
   a pan, low heat, cover with a lid for 5 mins.
   Add a little milk if the sauce is too solid



Checklist


          STEP 1 - Start by slicing your
          chicken breast into cubes then
          season with salt, Italian herbs,
          parsley, chilli flakes, garlic powder,
          paprika and olive oil. Mix
          thoroughly till well coated


          STEP 2 - Measure out your pasta
          then get water in a pot. Get it to a
          bowl then add your pasta in and
          boil for 8-10 mins till al dente. Once

   800g Raw White Potatoes peeled and diced

          salt and garlic powder. Use a
          spatula to mix everything till
          smooth and combined


          STEP 5 - Now place all your
          chicken thighs flat and evenly
          spread on a lined sheet pan. Now
          oven bake or air fry for 22-25mins
          at 200C / 400F. The chicken should
          come out golden and charred!


          STEP 6 - For the cream sauce get a
          pan on low heat, melt down some
          butter then add the spice mix
          consisting of the same spices used
          for the chicken. Toast the spices for
          2 mins in the butter


          STEP 7 - Add milk followed by
          cream cheese, parmesan cheese
          and chopped spinach. Mix gently
          till everything is melted, thickened

          and the spinach wilted


          STEP 8 - Finally when the sauce is
          ready, chop your chicken into
          delicious cubes then serve with the
          mashed potatoes topped with the
          sauce and ENJOY!
