# Dylan Shannon — 7 Things to Build Powerful Legs

Source: pasted by user (verbatim Dylan Shannon principles).

> If you're looking to build powerful legs then make sure you are implementing these 7 things weekly in your training plan.

1. **Sprint maximally** — all-out sprints with full recoveries. Sprints between 10–60 yards with a rest period of 60 seconds for every 10 yards sprinted.
2. **Jump high & far** — continuous vertical jumps, continuous broad jumps, kneeling jumps, seated jumps, skips, bounds, hops.
3. **Move weight violently** — Olympic lifts, weighted jumps.
4. **Squat heavy** — back squats, front squats, box squats, hack squats, Zercher squats.
5. **Hinge heavy** — Deadlifts, RDLs, stiff legged deadlifts.
6. **Get strong on one leg** — reverse lunges, rear foot elevated lunges, walking lunges.
7. **Build the hammies** — glute ham raises, low back extensions, seated curls, lying curls.

> Build speed, explosive power and strength by implementing these steps in your routine.

## Sample training day

1. Maximal sprints 6x20 yards (2 min rest)
2. Continuous broad jumps 3x5
3. Power clean 3x3
4. Back Squats 4x5
5. RDL 3x6
6a. DB Bulgarians 2x8
6b. Seated hamstring curls 2x8
